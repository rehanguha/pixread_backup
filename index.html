What PixRead Offers ?

PixRead can perform various functions according to the user requirements. PixRead has now multiple functionalities according to the input image and the desired output.
"Extract text from an image (OCR)" is the classic work of PixRead where it extracts text from the image and search online.
"Barcodes, QR Codes ..." is the new feature where it will extract the data from the 2D Codes and it will be searched.
